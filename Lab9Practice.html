Cepeliniai

Cepeliniai is one of my favorite Lithuanian dishes. It consists of a potato paste exterior, wrapped around a pork sausage or mushroom mixture at the center, topped with bacon bits and sour cream. This dish my family hold an entire party over once a year, where we make it together. I personaly prefer the potk option, but the mushroom version is very good too.

Kugelis

Kugelis is a lithuanian staple. I esentialy is a just potato bake with bacon bits on the inside and outside, and of course topped with sour cream. Im not a massive fan of this dish personaly. But it is the most popular of the lithuanian dishes

Spurgos

Spurgos is a donut like pastry. they are large dounut holes that contain curd cheese inside. when finished frying them, you add powdered sugar on top. this is my favorite lithuanian desert.
